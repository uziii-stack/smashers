Who We Are

Twenty Years. One Obsession.

CricketPro Gear started on a simple premise: the equipment available to club cricketers, academy players, and semi-professionals simply wasn't good enough. The bats cracked after one season. The bags fell apart in the rain. We decided to fix that.

From our first workshop in 2004, we focused on materials science, real field testing, and the kind of honest feedback that only comes from players who train every day.
